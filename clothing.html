CLOTHING & LAYERING

Stay warm, dry, and invisible. The complete guide to hunting apparel.

The Layering System

The key to comfort in any weather is layering. Each layer serves a specific purpose.

01

Base Layer

Moisture Management

Wicks sweat away from your skin to keep you dry. Never cotton!

Best Materials:

  • Merino wool
  • Synthetic polyester
  • Silk
02

Mid Layer

Insulation

Traps body heat to keep you warm. Thickness varies by temperature.

Best Materials:

  • Fleece
  • Down
  • Synthetic insulation
03

Outer Layer

Protection

Shields from wind, rain, and snow. Should be breathable.

Best Materials:

  • Gore-Tex
  • Soft shell
  • Hardshell
