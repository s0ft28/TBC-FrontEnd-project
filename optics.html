Understanding Binocular Numbers

10 X 42

10x

Magnification Power

42mm

Objective Lens Size

The first number is magnification—how many times closer objects appear. The second is lens diameter in millimeters—larger lenses gather more light for brighter images.

Recommended Sizes by Use

Western hunting, open terrain

High magnification, excellent light

15x56

Maximum power, tripod recommended

Rifle Scope Essentials

Magnification Range

Variable scopes like 3-9x are most versatile. Low power for close shots, high for long range. Fixed power scopes are simpler but less flexible.

3-9x40 is the most popular hunting scope configuration

Reticle Types

Duplex reticles are simple and effective. BDC reticles help with bullet drop. MOA/MIL dots allow precise holdovers at known distances.

Start with a simple duplex reticle

Eye Relief

The distance from your eye to the scope. 3-4 inches is ideal for hunting rifles to prevent scope eye from recoil. so try to use that kind of scope to comfort your eyes.

Always verify eye relief before purchasing
